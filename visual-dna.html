Frequently Asked Questions

How is Visual DNA different from brand guidelines?

Brand guidelines are rules. Visual DNA is systematic intelligence. Guidelines tell you "use this blue." Visual DNA teaches you when to use which blue and why, through frameworks that work across infinite contexts.

What is the typical timeline?

Brand DNA Foundation: 2-3 weeks. Component Library Systems: 3-4 weeks. Complete Design System: 6-8 weeks. Timeline depends on complexity and revision cycles.

Do you work with our existing designers?

Absolutely. Visual DNA frameworks integrate with your team's workflow. We are not replacing your designers; we are giving them systematic tools that make their work more effective and consistent.

What if we need to expand the system later?

Visual DNA is built for evolution. The systematic foundation makes expansion logical and consistent. Additional components, new media formats, brand extensions: all flow naturally from the core framework.
